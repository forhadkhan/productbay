How It Works

Three simple steps from installation to a live, interactive product table on your store.

1

Install & Activate

Upload ProductBay through the WordPress Plugins screen and activate it. Make sure WooCommerce is active — that's the only requirement.

2

Build Your Table

Open the ProductBay dashboard, launch the 5-step wizard, and configure products, columns, and design exactly the way you want — with live preview at every step.

3

Embed Anywhere

Copy your table's shortcode and paste it into any page, post, or block. Your interactive product table goes live instantly.

Creation Wizard

1 Setup Name your table, choose a product source (All, Category, Sale, or Specific IDs), and apply query modifiers like stock status, price range, and default sort order.
